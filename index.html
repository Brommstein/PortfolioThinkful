About Me

Born in a small town called Springtown, I grew up with very few people around me and due to that, have gained a fascination towards video games.

After graduating high school, I left my hometown and proceeded to live in Houston for two years. After experiencing a bit of city life, my family purchased a small house in Katy and settled down.

Living in Katy now allowed me to proceed with my dream of becoming a video game developer and so I started to attend school. Sadly though, due to financial issues, I was not able to complete my full degree with my community college.

Now I am working harder than ever along with a few of my best friends to solidify my knowledge of coding and create a possible future for ourselves!
